Instructions

Hey there!

Geofeelings is a web application where you can share your mood status among others. Take your time and explore.

1. Register

After registering, you can click at one of the smileys that refers to your current mood and put in a short message.

2. Chat with new people

In the right corner you can send messages to other people and check out how they feel and where they're hanging out!

3. Timelines

Click on a pin to check the latest posts of your favorite user!

4. Explore

There is a lot more to explore to find out yourself. Tell your friends, register and see what else you can do.
